Our history

The company Favaro Hnos. S.R.L. It is dedicated to the chestnut industry since 2014, it is located in the city of Cobija in the Pando department, on the road to the Mejillones community. The company processes its own raw material as well as offers Service outsourcing to institutions, cooperatives that are dedicated to the chestnut sector.

The Company Favaro HNOS SRL works with its own capital, and produces the following types of chestnuts, such as Large, Medium, Midget, Tiny, Chipped and Broken, Broken D achieving acceptance of its product in the international market.

Family Favaro Hnos S.R.L

MISION

To be leaders in the markets in which we participate, ensuring our permanence through a quality service and increasing productivity of our product benefiting customers and suppliers, workers and the community in general.

VISION

To have a consolidated company and leader in quality with a perspective of continuous growth with all the qualified personnel working in an environment that motivates them to achieve the objectives set for the pride of all the members of the Company.

SOCIAL RESPONSABILITY

The Favaro company provides almond shell and powder to different productive sectors of the department of Pando, promoting the beautification of the city's gardens.
