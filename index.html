History

As background, the Favaro family has more than 40 years of experience in the field of benefited from almonds, starting Mr. Valerio Favaro Sassi and his wife Lourdes Rojas Colonel, father and mother of Emilio Favaro Rojas during his career with the company Lourdes SRL Located in the department of Beni Ciudad de Riberalta, the family decided to venture into the Department of Pando Founding the company Favaro Hnos SRL in the city of Cobija, Carretera Mejillones, thus being the direct partners, Emilio Favaro Rojas and his wife Miriam Lazo de Favaro and his children Valerio Favaro Lazo, Lourdes Pamela Favaro Lazo, Mariana Favaro Lazo de Destre and Daniela Favaro Lazo current General Manager of the company Favaro Hnos srl
